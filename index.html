About Us

Namaste sabhi ko! Aaj hum baat karenge ek aise baat par, jo humare jeevan ka ek hissa ban gayi hai, lekin hum usse zyada seriously nahi lete – aur wo hai "Muth Marna!" Haan haan, sun ke mat ghabraiye, main kuch ajeeb nahi bolne wala hoon. Mera matlab hai "muth maarna" ek zabardast tareeke se apne life ko jeene ki baat! Ab aap soch rahe honge, yeh "muth marna" kya hota hai? Main aapko bata du ki muth marna ka matlab hai – apne dimaag ko thoda relax karna, apne routine se bahar nikal kar kuch hasi-mazak karna, thodi masti karna. Koi kaam karte waqt aise breaks lena zaroori hote hain! Jaise hum school mein padhte the aur teacher humse kehti thi, "Beta, tumhe achha karna hai, muth maarna mat!" Kya hote the hum tab! Hum sochte the ki yeh kya hawa mein udne wali baat hai? "Muth" ka kaise sambandh padayi se ho sakta hai? Magar ab samajh aata hai ki apne kaam ko seriously lena aur thoda relax hona bhi zaroori hota hai. Aur ek cheez yaad rakhna! Agar aap life mein apni "muth" ko thoda aur "jordar" karoge, matlab zindagi mein thodi masti daaloge, thodi hasi-mazak karoge, toh har din kaam karne ki taqat milegi. Samajhdari se "muth maarna" ek creative soch hai, jisse zindagi thodi light ho jaati hai! Ab, ek baat suno! Aaj kal hum sabhi har waqt busy rehte hain. Office, school, ghar, har jagah humari "muth maarne" ki power kaafi kam hoti ja rahi hai. Par jab hum thoda chill karte hain, apne friends ke saath ghoomte hain, ya apni favorite comedy movie dekhte hain, tabhi hum apni "muth" ko asli maza de paate hain!
